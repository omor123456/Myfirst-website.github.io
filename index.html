Today's topic is "How to protect yourself from hackers?"

How can hackers find me?

Anyone who uses a computer connected to the internet has a risk of getting hacked by a hacker. First of all, we need to know about how hackers get the info from us. A hacker might reach out to us by sending spam emails, calling from an unknown number, or simply  clinking to an unusual link/downloading files  (which can contain some type of virus or malware. After that, computer hackers can also try to access your computer and private information directly if you are not protected by a firewall. By doing this, they can monitor our personal data. 

What are things that a hacker can do to me?

  • Hijack your usernames and passwords
  • Steal your money and open credit card and bank accounts in your name
  • Request new account Personal Identification Numbers
  • Make purchases
  • Use and abuse your Social Security number
  • Obtain any contact info with others
  • Track our location etc.
